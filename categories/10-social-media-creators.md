## 🔗 How to Follow & Engage

### **Twitter/X Best Practices**

1. **Create a Security-Focused Account**
   - Professional profile
   - Clear bio mentioning interests
   - Security focus/interests listed

2. **Follow Smart**
   - Mix of red team and blue team accounts
   - Diverse perspectives and experience levels
   - Researchers and practitioners

3. **Engage Meaningfully**
   - Ask thoughtful questions
   - Share interesting findings
   - Participate in discussions
   - Give credit when sharing others' work

4. **Build Your Network**
   - Join security communities
   - Participate in Twitter Spaces (audio discussions)
   - Engage with security events/conferences

---

### **YouTube Optimization**

1. **Subscribe & Bell**
   - Enable notifications for new uploads
   - Create playlists for topics
   - Mark videos for offline viewing

2. **Engagement**
   - Like and comment on videos
   - Ask questions for clarification
   - Share helpful resources
   - Support creators

3. **Learning Path**
   - Create viewing playlists by skill level
   - Follow series from beginning to end
   - Take notes while watching
   - Revisit complex topics

---

### **Mastodon Best Practices**

1. **Create Account**
   - Choose privacy-focused instance
   - Write descriptive bio
   - Set profile picture

2. **Discover Content**
   - Follow security hashtags (#cybersecurity #infosec #appsec)
   - Use instance local timeline
   - Follow recommendations from others
   - Join security-focused communities

3. **Engage**
   - Participate in discussions
   - Share relevant findings
   - Boost (share) important posts
   - Respect community guidelines

## ⚠️ Critical Tips for Following Security Content

✅ **Verify Information**: Cross-reference claims with official sources
✅ **Understand Context**: Techniques shared are for authorized testing only
✅ **Stay Ethical**: Remember to use knowledge responsibly
✅ **Balance Perspectives**: Follow both red and blue team voices
✅ **Avoid Misinformation**: Be critical of sensational claims
✅ **Engage Respectfully**: Cybersecurity community is collaborative
✅ **Give Credit**: Always attribute sources and creators
